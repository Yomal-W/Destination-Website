Welcome to Rome

Neighborhoods, Local Communities, Cultural Practices

Monti

In the centre of Rome, close to famous sites like the Colosseum and the Roman Forum, is the charming and historic neighbourhood of Monti. The distinctive fusion of bohemian spirit and old-world charm in Monti makes it stand out. Its cobblestone streets wind through a labyrinth of little alleyways, presenting a hidden gem of antique shops, artisan studios, and quaint cafes. Monti is a centre for modern Roman culture since it is a haven for artists, thinkers, and free spirits. You'll come across bright street art, regional art galleries, and a variety of unique dining establishments, from trattorias to fusion restaurants, as you meander through its lanes. The vibrant aspect of this area is further accentuated by its function as a hub for musical and cultural events. For visitors looking for a genuine Roman experience with a contemporary twist, Monti is a must-visit location since it perfectly captures the essence of Rome's past and present.

Testaccio

Rome's Testaccio neighbourhood is located on the southern bank of the Tiber River and has a long history. Testaccio, which was formerly an industrial area, has evolved into a culinary and cultural mecca. This area is well known for its delicious selection of traditional dishes, including the well-known tripe made in the style of Rome. For those seeking fresh produce, regional cheeses, and mouth watering street food treats, Mercato Testaccio, the city's food market, is a must-visit. Along with its culinary highlights, Testaccio is home to places for contemporary art, like the Mattatoio, a former abattoir turned cultural hub that hosts exhibitions and events. Residents and guests congregate for festivals and celebrations, which reflects the neighborhood's vibrant atmosphere and strong sense of community. Testaccio successfully combines the old and the new, making it an alluring destination for travellers interested in experiencing Rome's rich culinary heritage and changing cultural landscape.
